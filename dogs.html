Fun Facts About Dogs

We all know that dogs are "man's best friend." This is because they are loving, adorable, and protective animals. There are lots of interesting facts about dogs that make them special. Today, we're going to list some of those amazing traits that make our furry friends so awesome!

1. Whiskers Help Dogs See In The Dark

Your dog's whiskers have nerves that send signals to their brain in order to help them whenever their visibility is low. This is super important when they are in an enclosed space and need to navigate their way out! Luckily, they are always recieving sensory messages through their whiskers.

2. A Dog's Nose Can Be Used As Its Fingerprint

This is an amazing fact because many pet owners have been able to track their missing pet using their DNA! Dogs have unique nose patterns that make it possible to track where they are on special mobile apps, such as the IAMS app, NOSEID.

Dogs Are As Smart As 2-Year Olds

Dogs are very intelligent creatures. They are capable of learning many words and phrases. Most research has compared their learning abilities to todlers. The average pup can recognize around 165 words! That's awesome!
