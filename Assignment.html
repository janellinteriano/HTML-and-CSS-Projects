Kittens General Information

Here are some cool facts about kittens that you may not have heard of.

Kittens Are Born Blind.

Most kittens begin to open their eyes around 7-10 days after birth. Their vision remains blurry until they are about 10 weeks old. This is when their vision starts to fully develop!

All Kittens Are Born With Blue Eyes

That's right! All kittens once had blue eyes, even the darkest eyed kitties! Their eye colors develop as they get older, until they have their own personal eye color at 8 weeks old.
